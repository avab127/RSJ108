How to Balance Life and College as a Journalism Student

Assembly time: 30 minutes

Effectiveness: 100%

As a journalism major, deadlines can crowd your head and even get you off track. Writing down assignments, deadlines, and commitments, as well as maintaining a social life can help you to feel like a more peaceful and productive version of yourself.

Things You'll Need

  • A class schedule
  • Sticky notes
  • A pen
  • A calendar

1. Plan your schedule. Write down all your classes, deadlines, and commitments.

2. Stay organized. Use sticky notes and calendars to keep track of assignments and story ideas.

3. Take care of yourself. Make time for rest, friends, and activities outside of school.
